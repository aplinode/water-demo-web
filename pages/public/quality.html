Our Journey

The PureAqua Cycle

1. Water Collection

Our process begins with sustainable extraction from deep-earth aquifers. Each batch is preliminarily tested for mineral balance before entering our facility.

water_drop
filter_alt

2. RO Filtration

Advanced multi-stage Reverse Osmosis membranes remove 99.9% of dissolved solids, chemicals, and heavy metals, leaving only pure hydration molecules.

3. UV Treatment

Our Ultra-Violet sterilization chambers neutralize biological contaminants. This chemical-free disinfection ensures the water remains crisp and safe without adding odors.

wb_sunny
biotech

4. Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes hourly laboratory analysis. We monitor pH levels, TDS (Total Dissolved Solids), and microbiological purity to exceed global bottled water regulations.

5. Packaging

Bottles are rinsed, filled, and capped in a sterile, automated environment. Our hands-free process prevents any secondary contamination during the final sealing stage.

6. Delivery

Our professional logistics team ensures that your water arrives on time and in perfect condition. Real-time tracking keeps you informed from plant to doorstep.
