Need more help?

Ordering your PureAqua hydration is simple. You can place an order through our "Order Now" button in the navigation bar, use our mobile application, or call our 24/7 hotline. For corporate accounts, we offer a dedicated dashboard for bulk management.

Web Portal Mobile App

We pride ourselves on precision. Standard delivery occurs within 2-4 hours of order placement within metropolitan areas. Subscription deliveries are scheduled according to your preferred morning or evening window to ensure you never run out of pure water.

PureAqua provides various sizes to fit your specific needs:

  • Premium 5-Gallon (18.9L): Ideal for home and office dispensers.
  • Family 3-Gallon (11.3L): Perfect for small kitchen counters.
  • Personal Case: 24 x 500ml bottles for on-the-go hydration.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and automated bank transfers for subscription plans. Monthly invoicing is available for our corporate clients.

Choose from our flexible subscription tiers: Basic Hydrate (4 bottles/mo), Family Flow (10 bottles/mo), or Corporate Pro (Unlimited/Custom). Subscriptions include complimentary dispenser cleaning twice a year.
